Welcome to GRIMDARK Stratagem Generator

📜 Import List: Click "Import List" to load your .html, .rosz or .ros (40k App/New Recruit/BattleScribe) file. The app will automatically detect your units, Keywords, and Faction, outputting your personalized Stratagems.

⚙️ Display & Favorites: Sign in with a Username profile to sync your Favorites. Toggle "Display Filters" to show Core, Faction, or exclusively your Favorites selections.

📱 Install App: You can install this app directly to your mobile device's home screen for permanent offline use via your browser's "Add to Home Screen" command!
